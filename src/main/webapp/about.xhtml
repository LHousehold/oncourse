So what exactly is On Course?



On Course is a learning platform created for instructors and students alike

As students ourselves, the developers of On Course are no strangers to the confusion that comes from the sheer volume of learning material thrown at us every day. Things like



Hard Copy Textbooks

Course Handouts

Digital Textbooks and Resources

Too Many Learning Management Sites

And so much more...



On Course is a place where instructors can place all of the learning material a class is going to need throughout the semester, in a sequential, easy to read manner

We're just interested in helping instructors give students exactly what they need
